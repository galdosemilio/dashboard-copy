To setup your clinic's custom mobile app, CoachCare will need information about your clinic and some of the customizations you would like. Please make an effort to provide the requested information in a timely manner. The faster we receive your clinic's details, the sooner we will be able to release your app.

Please email implementation@coachcare.com with your responses to the following items:

Settings and Preferences

  1. App Name
    • This will be the name your clients will search via the App Store (iOS) or the Google Play Store (Android).
    • Please limit to 12 characters.
  2. Short description of your services
    • Please limit this description to up to 8o characters.
    • This will be shown on the App Store (iOS) and the Google Play Store (Android)
  3. Full description of your services
    • Please limit this description to up to 4000 characters.
    • This will be shown on the App Store (iOS) and the Google Play Store (Android)
  4. Link to your clinic's website
  5. Supplements Optional
    • A listing of all supplements used in your clinic, along with daily dosage.
    • For example:
      • MultiVitamin, 2 per day
      • Calcium, 1 per day
  6. Schedule structure Optional
    • A listing of all meeting types used at your clinic, with length(s) of each appointment
    • For example:
      • Initial Consultation, 60 minutes
      • Weekly Consultation, 30 minutes
      • Telephone Checkin, 15 minutes

Design

  1. High-resolution app logo
    • OPTION A: Submit your own logo
      • Size: 1024px Width by 1024px Height for the App Store (iOS) and the Google Play Store (Android).
      • Preferred Format: the file must be in .AI, .SVG, or .EPS format.
    • OPTION B: Allow CoachCare to build your clinic's logo
      • The cost is $250.00
      • CoachCare design team will create your logo based on your clinic website and/or notes sent by clinic liason. You will be provided with three proofs to choose from. Edits/changes to any of these proofs may incur additional charges.
  2. Featured image
    • OPTION A: Submit your own featured image
      • Size: 1024px Width by 500px Height.
      • Preferred Format: png format.
      • This will be displayed on the Google Play Store (Android).
        Example of a feature image in the Google Play Store (Google Maps App)
    • OPTION B: Use CoachCare's featured image
  3. Primary and secondary colors
    • OPTION A: Submit your own colors
      • Format: primary and secondary colors must be in either HEX or RGB format.
      • For Example:
        • (HEX) Primary color: #f05d5c, Secondary color: #514d4a
        • (RGB) Primary color: 240,93,92, Secondary color: 81,77,74
    • OPTION B: Allow CoachCare to choose your clinic's colors
